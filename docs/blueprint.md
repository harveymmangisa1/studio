# **App Name**: StockPaEasy

## Core Features:

- Product Creation: Products are created with name, category, SKU, cost, selling price, and stock quantity.
- Product Display: Products can be viewed and filtered by a variety of fields. 
- Sales Invoices: Create sales invoices with line items, tax, and discounts. Sales reporting by day/week/month
- Expense Logging: Log business expenses with category, amount, and date to show on profit/loss summaries
- Dashboard: Simple dashboard to show key values and summaries, such as todayâ€™s sales or low stock.
- AI-Powered Reorder Suggestions: AI-powered suggestions for products to reorder, based on historical sales data; the AI tool considers seasonality, product demand, and supplier lead times to generate intelligent reorder proposals.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Slate Blue (#6A5ACD) to bring a sense of professionalism and trustworthiness.
- Background color: Light gray (#F0F2F5), offering a clean, neutral backdrop that's easy on the eyes for long work sessions.
- Accent color: Soft Lavender (#E6E6FA) to highlight interactive elements and important notifications.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif for clear and readable UI text.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' for a clear, readable style
- Simple and consistent icons to visually represent product categories, actions, and reports.
- A clean, card-based layout that will promote clarity and organization.
- Subtle transitions to confirm data saves and update stock quantities, making user interactions feel polished.
