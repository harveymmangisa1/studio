# StockPaEasy User Guide

Welcome to StockPaEasy! This guide will help you get started with the core features of the application.

## 1. Dashboard

The dashboard is the first page you see when you log in. It provides a quick overview of your business, including:

*   **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):** Total revenue, sales, products, and customers.
*   **Alerts & Notifications:** Low stock warnings and pending orders.
*   **Recent Activity:** A feed of the latest sales, stock movements, and new customers.

## 2. Inventory

The inventory page allows you to manage your products.

*   **View Products:** See a list of all your products, including their stock status, SKU, and price.
*   **Add a Product:** Click the "Add Product" button to open a form where you can enter the details of a new product.
*   **Edit a Product:** Click the three dots next to a product and select "Edit" to modify its details.
*   **Delete a Product:** Click the three dots next to a product and select "Delete" to remove it from your inventory.

## 3. Sales

The sales section is where you manage your customer invoices.

*   **View Invoices:** See a list of all your sales invoices, including the customer, date, status, and amount.
*   **Create a New Invoice:** Click the "New Invoice" button to go to the invoice creation page.
    *   Select a customer.
    *   Add line items by selecting products and specifying quantities.
    *   The subtotal, tax, and total are calculated automatically.
*   **Mark as Paid:** For unpaid invoices, click the three dots and select "Mark as Paid" to record a payment.

## 4. Customers

The customers page allows you to manage your customer database.

*   **View Customers:** See a list of all your customers.
*   **Add a Customer:** Click the "Add Customer" button to add a new customer.
*   **Edit a Customer:** Click the three dots next to a customer and select "Edit" to update their information.
*   **Delete a Customer:** Click the three dots next to a customer and select "Delete" to remove them.

## 5. Expenses

The expenses page is where you can track your business expenses.

*   **View Expenses:** See a list of all your expenses.
*   **Add an Expense:** Click the "Add Expense" button to log a new expense. This will also create the necessary accounting entries.

## 6. Reports

StockPaEasy provides several financial reports to help you understand your business performance.

*   **Profit & Loss Statement:** Shows your revenue, costs, and profit over a selected period.
*   **Balance Sheet:** Provides a snapshot of your company's assets, liabilities, and equity.
*   **AR Aging Report:** Shows a breakdown of your unpaid invoices by how long they have been overdue.

## 7. Audit Log

The audit log provides a complete history of all actions taken within the application. This is useful for security and compliance purposes.
